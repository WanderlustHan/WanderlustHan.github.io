About Me

Peng Han is currently a Ph.D. candidate in Computer Science, at the King Abdullah University of Science and Technology (KAUST), Saudi Arabia. He is under the supervision of Prof. Xiangliang Zhang. Prior to that, he was a Research Associate in the School of Computer Science and Engineering, Nanyang Technological University (NTU), Singapore. He received his M.E. in Computer Science from Renmin University of China (RUC) in 2017.
